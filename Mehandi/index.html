What We Do

Our Service

At Bittu Mehndi Art, we offer a diverse range of mehndi services tailored to suit every occasion and preference. With over 15 years of experience, our skilled artisans bring creativity and precision to each design, ensuring a memorable experience for our clients.

Portrait Mehandi

Capture your cherished memories forever with our exquisite Portrait Mehandi service. Our talented artists skillfully replicate your favorite portraits onto your skin, creating a unique and personalized adornment that celebrates your special moments.

Legs Mehandi

Adorn your legs with intricate henna designs that add an extra touch of elegance to your overall look. Our Bittu mehandi art service OR Legs Mehandi service offers a variety of patterns and styles to complement your attire and elevate your style.

Wedding Mehndi

Elevate your wedding day with exquisite bridal mehndi designs that capture the essence of tradition and romance.Our Bittu Mehndi Art artists craft mesmerizing adornments that perfectly complement your bridal ensemble.

Special Occasions

Celebrate life's special moments with the timeless allure of mehndi. Whether it's an engagement, baby shower, or festive gathering, our skilled artisans at Bittu Mehndi Art create captivating designs that add a touch of elegance and charm to your festivities.

Corporate Events

Make a lasting impression at your corporate events with our professional mehndi services. From company celebrations to promotional events, we offer bespoke designs that reflect your brand identity and leave a memorable mark on attendees.

Rajwadi Mehndi

Transport yourself to the grandeur of royal traditions with our Rajwadi Mehndi service. Inspired by regal motifs and patterns, our artists create intricate designs that perfect for brides seeking a touch of majestic charm on their special day.

Simple Mehndi Bride

For the bride who prefers understated elegance, our Simple Mehndi Bride service offers delicate and minimalistic designs that enhance your natural beauty. Embrace simplicity without compromising on style with our expertly crafted mehndi designs.

Private Sessions

Indulge in the luxury of personalized mehndi artistry with our private sessions. Perfect for intimate gatherings or pampering sessions,Our artist cater to your preferences, creating bespoke designs that reflect your individual style and personality.
